# Customer-Pricing-Platform
Customer &amp; Pricing Platform is an integrated web-based application designed to centralize and simplify the management of business-critical operations including customer data, product catalogs, complex pricing strategies, and sales order processing.

With a robust Python backend (leveraging SQLAlchemy for relational data models) and a modern web front end, the platform enables businesses to:

- Store, retrieve, and update comprehensive customer and product information
- Define multi-level pricing structures (standard, custom price lists, and product-specific rates)
- Create, manage, and audit detailed customer ordersâ€”including tax calculation, subtotals, and order line items
- Seamlessly generate sales reports and access historical order information

## Features
- Centralized customer and product management
- Support for base and custom price lists
- End-to-end sales order creation and tracking
- Tax, subtotal, and grand total calculations
- Historical sales and product data reporting

## Technology Stack
- Python backend powered by SQLAlchemy and Eel
- Web-based frontend for intuitive user interaction

Ideal for organizations that require flexibility in pricing, centralized order tracking, and an all-in-one solution for managing customers, products, and pricing.
